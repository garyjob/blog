The pre-requisite for equitable distribution of wealth is predicated on equitable access to the means of production and distribution of goods and services

failing that the market would tend towards a monopoly or an oliogopoly

There is a positive correlation between the variety of goods/services and the general level of accessibility the population has to means of production and distribution
