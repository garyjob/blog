Money is temporal highly liquid store of value. Differing attitudes towards money leads to differing usages

 	- Safety/Survival - money is used in exchange for food, shelter and peace of mind
 	- Power (strictly as the ability to get things done) - money is treated one of the many tools that could be deployed to get from point A to point B. Power is also in itself a means to an ends.
 	- Pleasure - money is used purely for hedonistic purposes
 	- Status - money is seen as a means of ego gratification in relation to how one is perceived by others
