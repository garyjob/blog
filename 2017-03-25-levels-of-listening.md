Levels of listening mentioned by Colli discussed during the management consulting session

 	- ignoring what the other person has to say
 	- pretenfing to listen to what the other person has to say
 	- selectively listening to what the other person has to say
 	- listening to what the other person has to say with the intention of responding
 	- actually listening to what the other person is saying to fully understand the context and content of the communication
