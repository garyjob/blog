Farming insights: Will and Dean

December 10, 2017 Uncategorized

Will on soil management

- till and water land
- wait for one week
- pull out grass and plant seeds
- gives seeds a head start

Dean: worm and composting

- keep three containers on top of a single one at the bottom
- put worms in it.
- Worms will migrate upwards to where the food is
- lower containers will only be left with soil
- by worms from amazon
- Home Depot sells fake soil to aid water drainage
- can setup hydroponics with fish as s system
- buy a pump to pump water onto soil where plants are growing
- shit from fish goes to becoming fertilizer
