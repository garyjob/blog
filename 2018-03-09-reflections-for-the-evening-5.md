Its important to learn to be ok not feeling ok in negotiations

the deeper meaning is to learn to sit equanimously with the effects of adrenaline and dopamine withdrawal coursing through the body

Every piece of communication contains an emotional component and a data component. To effectively respond to the piece of communication is to process and diffuse the emotional component while extracting the essential data to make a well calibrated decision.
