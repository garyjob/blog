The general hype about over Artificial intelligence is causing much fear over potential Judgement Day like events.

Much has been missed over the impact thus far of what should be more appropriately termed Augmented Intelligence with the recent surge in computing power. A.K.A allowing the N+1 person coming to the party to quickly access and get up to speed on the knowledge generated by the previous N people.
