An afternoon spent in Russian Ridge selecting a frame, observing nature and capturing it on paper.

While reality bombards us with an extremely rich analog of data, the activity of capturing reality in a drawing converts data in into its discrete format. Much information is lost in the process.

Such is the same with other aspects of one's personal experience.
