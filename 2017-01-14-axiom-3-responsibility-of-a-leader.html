Axiom: 3 responsibilities of a leader

January 14, 2017 Uncategorized

Three responsibilities of leadership

- To cause a vision and mission to have tangible result in the real world
- For the followers to have more growth, wealth and health with an upswing in fulfillment
- Self leadership - a commitment to constant self improvement

Methodology for focus: 6 key focus for each 6 weeks

Three steps to hire:

- character - to validate before hiring
- competence - hire for key strength and not for lack of weaknesses
- chemistry - trust your gut feeling about whether u would want to see a person day in day out

words matters

- focus on hi and goodbyes so that people don't feel like they are tools in your machine
