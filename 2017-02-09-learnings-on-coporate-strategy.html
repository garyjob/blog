Learnings on Corporate Strategy

February 09, 2017 Uncategorized

High level overview:

Strategy comes in 3 phases

- Analysis
- Synthesis
- Implementation

Analysis

List of tools/frameworks to use when gathering raw materials for synthesis stage

- SWOT analysis - Macro analysis
- Porter 5 Forces - Micro analysis
- BCG Matric - Portfolio analysis
- Trends - Future
- Business Model Generation

Synthesis

- Mission: Core Business - what do we do

We buy and sell real estate for a profit

- Vision: How we see ourselves in the future:

Attributes:

Set with a specific date in mind
- Measurable
- Challenging
- KISS - Keep It Simple and Stupid

- Example: We plan to manage USD100 million worth of funds in the Single Family Home Real Estate Space in US by year 2020

- Values:

Description: Core Principle we will not compromise on while we pursue our goals:
- Example: Accuracy and Timeliness in our decision making

- Priority List:

Description: a list of milestones with corresponding expected dates of accomplishment that we need to hit for us to get from where we are not to where we want to be
- Example:

Mar 2017: Stabilize data pipelining
- April 2017: Finalize MVP for prediction models
- July 2017: Close first round of funding USD500K
- Mar 2018: Finished deploying funds
- July 2018: Close second round of funding USD1,500,000
- July 2019: Close second round of funding USD3,000,000
- July 2020: Closed X round of funding USD 5,000,000

Implementation

- Action Plan
- Balance Score Card
- Scrum
- OKRs
- Design Thinking
- Lean Methodology
