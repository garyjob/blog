 	- To be mindful 24hrs a day
 	- Thoughts are a naturally occurring phenomena
 	- The importance is not to chase away those thoughts
 	- The importance is to observe and become aware of those thoughts
 	- There is only now
 	- The most important person is the person right before you
 	- The most important pursuit is make the person standing at your side happy
 	- Subject cannot exist independently of Object: you cannot be angry without being angry at something
 	- Everything is inter-dependent and impermanent
