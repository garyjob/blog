## South facing entrance

Understood the practical reason behind FengShui emphasis on South facing entrance after reading up on farming and making the connections.

In the northern hemisphere the sun rises not from the east but from the south east. A south facing entrance means the household gets fresh sunlight in the morning but does not get too much sunlight during noon. Household this avoids getting overheated
