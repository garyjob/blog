## Conscious employees

 	- Character Trait

 	Unconditional response-ability - focus on aspects of situation that you can influence
 	- Essential integrity -

 	you will never have total control on outcomes (success/failure) but you will always have total control on how you choose to react (aligned with values/misaligned with values)
 	- integrity is about always reacting in alignment with your own set of values

 	- Ontological humility

 	mentally models are limited representations of reality -

 	biases my perception
 	- my beliefs are just hypothesis

 	- be mindful that you do not have full ownership to the truth
 	- when someone talks about a problem, she isn't describing an external reality by a concern about her well being

 	- Interpersonal

 	Authentic Communication

 	seek to understand what he has to say
 	- provide the necessary context
 	- clarify to make sure message is clearly communicated

 	- Constructive Negotiation

 	operational conflict:

 	limited resources,
 	- conflicting positions

 	- identify common underlying interest and negotiate from that position
 	- consensus

 	process consensus - tie breaking mechanism
 	- agree this is the best way though might not like the decision

 	- levels of negotiation

 	Personal - identity

 	manage other's fear of rejection
 	- don't try to make it alone

 	- Inter-personal - relationship
 	- Impersonal - object

 	- Impeccable coordination

 	Impeccable commitment

 	The goal is to obtain some service from receiver by a specific time
 	- Clarity of commitment: To expect and provide verbal acknowledgement of commitment with through well specified conditions

 	understand what is requested
 	- have the skills and resources to deliver
 	- convinced the opposite party can deliver
 	- willing to be held accountable for anticipating potential breakdown

 	- Missing commitments due - Golden Rule

 	treat others as you would yourself like to be treated
 	- Inform them early as you see failure to deliver
 	- Seek work around solution

 	- Impeccable praise

 	state very specifically what was the action that was being praised for
 	- state very clearly how his/her effort has helped you in terms of the results you want
 	- do it publicly

 	- Leaders:

 	walk the talk
 	- hold subordinates accountable

 	- Emotional Mastery

 	Self-awareness - know what is happening within yourself

 	can be cultivated and increased at will
 	- The center of a tornado is very still

 	- competencies / working with others

 	self awareness / recognition of the other's emotion

 	know its there

 	- self acceptance / acknowledgement of the other's emotion

 	without judgement

 	- self regulation / influence how the other react to his emotion

 	have control over how its express

 	- self inquiry / inquiry and find out his story

 	find out the root story

 	- self expression / listening

 	articulate the emotion and help the other understand

 	- unskillful styles

 	exploding outwards by blowing up at people
 	- stoic repression leads to imploding beyond a specific threshold

 	- skillful approach

 	acknowledge the existence of an emotion
 	- examine what mental model you hold lead to interpretation and subsequent emotional reaction
