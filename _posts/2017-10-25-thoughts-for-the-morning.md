**Intellectual agility**

 	- a whole hearted commitment to staying grounded in reality
 	- willingness to drop an entrenched position when a mistake is identified
 	- willingness to change strategy on the turn of a dime

**Under committing **

 	- To be very judicious in the deployment of personal time to any endeavour
 	- when possible seek to under achieve rather than over achieve
 	- keep time aside just to think and reflect
