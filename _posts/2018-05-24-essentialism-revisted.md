The principle of essentialism has been observed to rear its head in multiple domains

 	- Jazz improvisation under Mario to maximize the flavor conveyed using the minimal amount of keys
 	- Acting classes using the minimal amount of symbolism (props and actions) to momentarily suspend disbelief
 	- Coding using the minimal lines of code to deliver business value
 	- Writing using the minimal amount of words to convey the meaning
