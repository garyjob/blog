**Number 1 culprit: planning fallacy**

 	- natural human  bias - over-confidant

 	helps fights depression

 	- coordination overheads

 	projects are started by people who never end them

 	- procrastination
 	- impulse control

 	continous partial attention

**Solution**

 	- data is required to overcome planning fallacy

 	prevents lying to get what you want

 	realistic representation leads to no interest

 	- maintain historical data of over or under budgeting

 	punish if don't meet target
 	- reward if meet target

 	- use data/algorithms instead of human judgement to solve planning fallacy -
