A framework used to analysis a business model at a high level to understand strengths, weakness and feasibility of a business model.

 	- Left section provides visibility on components that contribute to COST
 	- Right section provides visibility on components that contribute to REVENUE

 	- Customer Segments - defined around pain points, needs and behavioral traits

 	Retail investors

 	working in the tech industry with funds to invest
 	- wants to have a balanced investment portfolio
 	- feels they already have a lot of financial exposure to technology
 	- wants exposure to less volatile asset class

 	- Value Proposition - what they value

 	stable cash flow
 	- low volatility
 	- Tax efficient

 	- Channels - how we reach them

 	Professional network
 	- SEO
 	- Website

 	- Customer Relationship - how we constantly stay in their face and keep them reminded of us

 	Wine and dine them
 	- Periodically follow up with them and send them messages on our progress
 	- How many hours do we plan to devote per month/week to stay in their face?

 	- **Revenue Streams - how much we expect to make from each customer?
**
 	- Key Resources

 	Engineers
 	- Investor relationship managers
 	- Marketeers
 	- Server Cost
 	- Lawyers
 	- Property managers

 	- Key Activities

 	Setup and Maintain crawlers/data pipeline
 	- Generate/qualify leads/limited partners
 	- Raise/Deploy/Exit funds
 	- Buy/Sell properties
 	- Rent out properties
 	- Maintain properties
 	- Disburse rental yield

 	- Key Partners

 	Property management companies
 	- Cloud solutions
 	- Real estate agents

 	- **Cost Structure - how much we expect our monthly operations to cost?**
