Upstream effects

 	- Always choose actions that will allow for greater levels of agency further down stream
 	- Entrainment - keeping training and putting into place process to reinforcement your training
 	- trajectory - measure and benchmark your progress. Always aspire to become better than where you are now

Toughness

 	- Mission toughness - energy should always be channeled towards intent
 	- Mental toughness - knowing what to do and doing it whether you want it or not
 	- physical toughness - physiological health and general strength

Aristotelian friendship

 	- Choose individuals that have impeccable characters to build associations with
 	- choose individuals that will constantly challenge you to aspire to become more
