Number 1 thing to avoid when managing products

September 13, 2017 Uncategorized

Spend time really thinking about distribution.

Avoid the Product Death Cycle by wantonly adding features to it on a whim, such malpractice increases the complexity for users as well as engineering overhead for developers.

It is important to be judicious what features/initiatives gets finally added and what must get discard.
